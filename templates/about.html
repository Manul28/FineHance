About

As college students, Adit, Aishwarya, Aditya Dhanraj and Aryan always thought we must track our expenses in college and make sure we spend it properly and that is why we came up with this MoneyManager Web App for our Software Engineering Class Project!
Functionalities of this app include: Managing your transactions and keeping track of all your expenses. It also provides a visual guide for the user to understand their expenditure through some data charts. You can also download your data in form of an excel sheet and compare your expenditure with your monthly budget.
App Version: v1.0.0
